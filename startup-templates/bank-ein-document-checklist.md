# Bank + EIN Document Checklist

## EIN
- [ ] LLC is approved by Florida first.
- [ ] Legal name exactly matches Articles of Organization.
- [ ] Responsible party information ready.
- [ ] Business mailing/principal address ready.
- [ ] Apply free directly with IRS.
- [ ] Save EIN confirmation letter PDF.
- [ ] Save any SS-4 notes used to apply.

## Bank account
Typical banks may ask for some or all of the following:
- [ ] Filed Articles of Organization.
- [ ] EIN confirmation letter.
- [ ] Operating agreement.
- [ ] Government ID.
- [ ] Business address / mailing address.
- [ ] Initial deposit.
- [ ] Ownership/control information.
- [ ] NAICS/business description.

## Account structure
- [ ] Operating checking.
- [ ] Tax reserve savings.
- [ ] Optional payroll/owner draw account later.
- [ ] Optional business credit card after bank account is active.

## Bookkeeping setup
- [ ] Chart of accounts.
- [ ] Bank feed connected.
- [ ] Receipt capture app/folder.
- [ ] Monthly close checklist.
- [ ] Tax reserve rule documented.
