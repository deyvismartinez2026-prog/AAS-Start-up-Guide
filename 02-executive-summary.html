Executive summary

The operating partner for measurable AI execution.

Advanced Agentic Systems (AAS) modernizes how work moves across teams, systems, decisions, and growth channels — founded and 100% owned by a service-disabled veteran, with SDVOSB certification through SBA VetCert planned in the first sixty days.

The problem

Organizations buy AI faster than they absorb it. Tools get licensed, pilots get demoed, and work still moves by hand — at a cost nobody has mapped or priced. As AI development commoditizes, the scarce skill is knowing what to build, proving its worth, and owning adoption to a measured result.

Why now

  • Federal agencies missed the new 5% SDVOSB goal in FY2025 ($28.6B awarded, 4.7%) — certified, AI-capable vendors are actively sought.
  • Federal AI demand is compounding: DoD's first dedicated AI budget line ($13.4B, FY2026); federal AI contracts up ~3.7× since 2022.
  • Commercial buyers, burned by stalled pilots, now pay for evidence: audits, governance, human-in-the-loop delivery.

The moat

A proprietary Operational Intelligence Platform productizes delivery: friction taxonomies, ROI math with documented sources, immutable readout snapshots. Every engagement makes the next faster and more defensible — compounding toward benchmarking (v2) and a GovCloud-ready SaaS posture (v3).

Two engines

Commercial (weeks-long cycles) funds the company and builds proof. Federal SDVOSB (quarter-long cycles) compounds position: set-asides, sole source to $5M, VA Vets First priority (23.6% of VA primes to SDVOSBs), OASIS+ SDVOSB on-ramp.
