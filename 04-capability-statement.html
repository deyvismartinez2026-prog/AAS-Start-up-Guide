Advanced Agentic Systems (AAS) modernizes how work moves. We map workflows, quantify the cost of friction, deploy agentic AI with humans in the loop, and stay accountable to measured outcomes. Map the workflow. Connect the tools. Expose the truth.

Core competencies

Workflow Intelligence Audits

Fixed-fee, four-week operational diagnostics: friction mapping, revenue-leakage analysis, opportunity scoring, and an executive readout with conservative and optimistic ROI scenarios. Every number traceable to a documented source.

Agentic AI & Workflow Automation

Production AI workflows: intake, routing, alerts, document handling, and reporting. Tool-agnostic integration with existing stacks; human-in-the-loop governance by default; 30-day stabilization with operator training.

AI Training, Onboarding & Adoption

Role-based AI enablement programs, prompt and tool playbooks, and governance guardrails that turn AI investment into everyday, policy-compliant practice across the workforce.

Managed AI Operations & Growth Systems

Ongoing monitoring, tuning, and expansion of deployed systems. Brand, web, AI chat and voice assistants, marketing automation, and CRM pipelines run as one measured operation.

Differentiators
  • Evidence before build. Engagements begin with quantified workflow truth — loaded-cost math, documented sources.
  • Fixed-fee, productized delivery. Defined artifacts, owners, and decision points. No open-ended hourly billing.
  • Human-in-the-loop governance. AI drafts; people approve. Numbers come from auditable models, never from generative output.
  • Founder-led senior delivery. Principal-level attention, backed by a proprietary platform with immutable reporting snapshots.
  • Service-disabled veteran-owned. SDVOSB set-aside and sole-source eligible upon SBA VetCert certification.
